About This Area

Built for Port Hueneme Homes

Port Hueneme sits right on the water, and the homes here — beach cottages, compact residentials near the harbor, and properties in the older neighborhoods off the beach — face the most demanding exterior conditions in Ventura County. Salt air, coastal moisture, and strong UV exposure can degrade a poorly finished exterior door in under three years. When we build for Port Hueneme, we use species with natural moisture resistance and apply marine-grade exterior finishing systems — multiple coats of penetrating oil and UV-blocking topcoat — so the door genuinely holds up to what it faces. We've built for clients here whose previous factory doors failed in two seasons and whose new custom doors are still performing years later.
