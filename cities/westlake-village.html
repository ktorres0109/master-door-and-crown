About This Area

Built for Westlake Village Homes

Westlake Village is home to some of the most distinctive residential properties in the greater Ventura County region — lakeside estates, contemporary custom homes, and established neighborhoods where architectural quality is a point of community pride. Custom woodwork here tends toward the ambitious: oversized entry doors with sidelites and transoms, full interior door packages throughout large homes, detailed crown moulding and built-ins, and barn-style room dividers for open-plan entertaining spaces. We've worked extensively in Westlake Village and take on projects of any scale, from a single entry door to a full carpentry scope across an entire home renovation.
