About This Area

Built for Santa Paula Homes

Santa Paula has one of the most intact collections of early-20th-century residential architecture in Ventura County — Victorian-influenced homes, craftsman bungalows, and colonial revivals built when wood trim and custom millwork were standard practice rather than a luxury. Renovating homes in Santa Paula often means matching existing moulding profiles, period-appropriate panel configurations, and historic casing details that no catalog door can replicate. We've built doors and trim packages for Santa Paula renovations that are invisible to a trained eye — matching old-growth wood profiles with contemporary materials and techniques. If you have a historic home in Santa Paula that needs a door or trim work that respects what's already there, this is exactly the kind of project we take on.
