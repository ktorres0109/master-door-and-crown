About This Area

Built for Ventura Homes

Ventura's housing stock spans coastal craftsman bungalows from the 1920s, mid-century ranches, Spanish revival properties near downtown, and newer builds in the hills above the city. The combination of marine air, salt exposure, and intense UV means exterior doors here need to be built with both aesthetics and durability in mind. Master Door and Crown builds for all of Ventura's architectural styles, with wood species and finish systems selected specifically for the coastal climate. If you're in Ventura and want a door that looks right for your home and holds up against the ocean air, we'll come take a look.
