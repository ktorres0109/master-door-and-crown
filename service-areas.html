Where We Work

Custom Wood Doors Throughout Ventura County, CA

We serve homeowners from the coast to the foothills — every city in Ventura County is in our service area. If you're here, we'll come to you.

Ventura County is a remarkably diverse place — coastal bungalows in Ventura, Spanish colonials in Ojai, contemporary builds in Thousand Oaks, historic farmhouses in Fillmore. Every architectural style calls for a different door, and we've built custom wood doors for all of them. Our in-home consultation process means we come to your city, see your home in context, and design a door that belongs there.

Cities We Serve

Find Your City

Click your city for more information about the types of homes and doors we build in your area.

Ventura →

Ventura's coastal homes range from 1920s craftsman bungalows to mid-century ranches — styles that call for solid wood entry doors with traditional panel profiles and weather-resistant finishes built for marine air and salt exposure.
Oxnard →

From Channel Islands Harbor to the Silver Strand, Oxnard homes near the water demand exterior doors built with extra attention to moisture and UV exposure. We build in teak, ipe, and mahogany for clients in the most exposed coastal locations.
Thousand Oaks →

Thousand Oaks is home to some of the most ambitious residential architecture in the county — contemporary estates, traditional colonials, and Spanish revival homes that reward a carefully designed entry door. We build for all of them.
Camarillo →

Camarillo's residential neighborhoods include established track homes ready for upgrades as well as custom builds in the foothills. We frequently work on interior door packages and trim upgrades that transform builder-grade homes into something special.
Simi Valley →

Simi Valley homeowners frequently request craftsman and ranch-style entry doors that complement the area's established neighborhood character. Barn doors have also become popular in the area's open-plan ranch renovations.
Moorpark →

Moorpark's newer developments and equestrian-adjacent properties call for doors with substance — oversized entry doors with bold profiles, and barn doors for spaces that connect to the outdoors. We've done both extensively in this community.
Santa Paula →

Santa Paula's historic downtown district includes many homes from the early 20th century — craftsman bungalows and Victorian-influenced properties that deserve historically appropriate wood doors and trim rather than modern substitutes.
Fillmore →

Fillmore retains a small-town character with working ranches and historic homes throughout the Santa Clara River Valley. We've built doors for renovations of historic farmhouses and Craftsman-era properties throughout this community.
Port Hueneme →

Port Hueneme's beachside and near-beach homes face some of the harshest conditions for exterior wood — persistent moisture, salt air, and coastal sun. We use marine-grade finishing processes and rot-resistant species for all exterior work here.
Ojai →

Ojai attracts homeowners who care deeply about craftsmanship and natural materials — exactly our kind of client. Spanish adobe, arts-and-crafts cottages, and contemporary hillside homes all present interesting door design challenges we love to solve.
Newbury Park →

Newbury Park's master-planned neighborhoods offer a great canvas for a custom entry door that sets a home apart. We work on both single-door replacements and full interior packages for homes throughout this community.
Oak Park →

Oak Park's gated communities and newer custom homes call for doors that make a statement — pivot doors, wide contemporaries, and craftsman designs with specific glass and hardware requirements we build to spec.
Westlake Village →

Westlake Village's lakeside estates and custom homes are some of the most ambitious residential projects in the county. We take on full-scope work here — entry doors, interior packages, crown moulding, and built-ins.
Somis →

Somis ranch properties and agricultural estates require doors built for real exposure — wide entries, durable species, finishes that hold up to the inland agricultural climate. We build for Somis properties of any scale.
