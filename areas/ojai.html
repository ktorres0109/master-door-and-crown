About This Area

Doors Built for Ojai's Distinct Architecture

Ojai attracts a particular kind of homeowner — people who are drawn to natural materials, handmade objects, and spaces that feel considered rather than assembled from a showroom. It's a community that understands the difference between a door that was built and a door that was manufactured. We build for exactly this kind of client.

Ojai's residential architecture reflects its history and landscape. Spanish colonial adobes with thick plaster walls and arched openings call for substantial, character-rich entry doors — often with arched tops, heavy hand-forged hardware, and warm wood tones that complement terracotta and white plaster. Craftsman bungalows in and around downtown Ojai deserve doors with the honest joinery and natural wood grain that defined that movement: no veneers, no filler, just solid wood craftsmanship.

Ojai's hillside properties and newer contemporary homes present a different opportunity — clean, minimal doors that frame views and connect indoor and outdoor living. We build in reclaimed and sustainably sourced woods for clients whose values extend to the materials in their home. Every door we've built in Ojai started with a conversation about the home's character and ended with a door that made the entrance better.
