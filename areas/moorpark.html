About This Area

Custom Doors for Moorpark Homes

Moorpark has grown significantly in recent decades, with newer tract developments and custom homes filling in the hillside terrain north of the 118. It's also a community with deep agricultural and equestrian roots — properties that connect to the land, value durability, and appreciate materials that carry meaning beyond their function.

Moorpark's newer custom homes frequently feature oversized entry openings that demand a door with proportional substance — a standard 3/0 door looks undersized in these entries. We build to any dimension, and we've constructed several wide-format entry doors for Moorpark properties where the architecture called for a statement entry that matched the scale of the home.

Barn doors are also popular in Moorpark — the community's outdoor-connected properties and open-plan homes are natural environments for sliding doors that blur the division between living and outdoor spaces. We build custom barn doors in character-grade oak, reclaimed pine, and other species that suit the rural-adjacent feel many Moorpark homeowners cultivate.
