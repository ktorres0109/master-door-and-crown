About This Area

Custom Doors for Simi Valley Homes

Simi Valley is a well-established family community with a mix of 1970s–1990s tract homes, newer construction in the east end, and some older properties in the hills that have distinct character. It's a city where homeowners invest in their properties for the long term — and upgrading a front door is one of the most impactful investments they can make.

Simi Valley's ranch and craftsman-influenced homes are a natural fit for solid wood doors — the material connects to the architectural language of these homes in a way that fiberglass and steel never do. We build craftsman-profile entry doors with divided-light windows, 5-panel raised-panel doors in natural oak, and contemporary doors for the area's newer builds that reflect the surrounding landscape's clean lines.

Barn doors have also gained significant popularity in Simi Valley's ranch renovations — the style suits open-plan interiors naturally, and a well-built custom barn door in white oak or reclaimed pine transforms a room divider into a design feature. We've done several in Simi Valley and welcome the work.
