About This Area

Handbuilt Doors for Fillmore Homes

Fillmore sits at the east end of the Santa Clara River Valley, a small agricultural community that has retained its character in a way that's increasingly rare in Southern California. Its older residential neighborhoods contain craftsman bungalows, early ranch properties, and historic farmhouses — buildings with honest construction and materials that modern replacements rarely honor.

Fillmore homeowners who want to restore or upgrade their entries face the same problem that historic homeowners throughout the county face: the manufactured door industry doesn't build for their homes. Non-standard openings, period-appropriate profiles, and the material quality needed for historic restoration just aren't available at a home improvement store. We come to Fillmore and build what the home actually needs.

For Fillmore's working ranches and agricultural properties, we also build practical barn doors and sliding panels — the functional kind, built with the material quality to last decades rather than years. A well-built sliding panel in Douglas fir or pine connects to the history of the land the same way the original agricultural structures did.
